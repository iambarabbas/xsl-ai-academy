The Claude Requirement

This workshop is built specifically on Claude, the AI assistant made by Anthropic. Claude is not the same as ChatGPT or other AI tools, and the session is designed around a specific Claude feature called Projects.

Claude Projects is what makes a personal AI agent possible. It creates a persistent workspace that holds your Project instructions, your knowledge files, and your context file across every conversation. Without Projects, every conversation starts from scratch and the agent concept breaks down entirely.

Projects is available on Claude Pro at $20 per month. A free Claude account is not sufficient for this session.

You will receive account setup instructions by email 48 hours before the workshop. Please complete setup before you arrive so we spend the full session building, not troubleshooting logins.
